Our Approach

Operator-friendly real estate. Conservative underwriting. Simple operations.

We focus on preventa (pre-sale) residential projects in Mexico City delegations with proven appreciation trajectories. Every project is selected for value-add potential, operational simplicity, and quality of life for end buyers.

Our underwriting starts from the downside: What does this project look like if sales velocity is 30% slower than projected? If construction costs rise 15%? Only projects that survive those scenarios make it into the portfolio.

Mexico City Market

Why CDMX, why now.

Mexico City's residential market continues to benefit from structural tailwinds: nearshoring-driven income growth, a chronic housing deficit in central delegations, and a professional class increasingly willing to pay a premium for well-designed urban living.

Benito Juárez and Iztacalco specifically offer the combination of metro access, walkability, and remaining land availability that drives appreciation in constrained urban markets.
